Alexandream
Online Library Management

Alexandream is a lexical combination of Alexandria and Dream. Alexandria is the name of the infamous bygone library, the Great Library of Alexandria. The meaning behind this name is an inspiration of knowledge comprehension and one’s respective dreams. Alexandream is also the place for all readers to perceive any of crucial information in the world in order to advance in their pursuit.

A library management system that provides many useful features helping us manage books and employees in library more easily and conveniently.
